After You Book

Here is what
to expect next.

  • Confirmation message

    You will receive a booking confirmation with your service, stylist, date, and time.

  • Prep instructions when applicable

    Some services may include prep guidance — such as arriving with detangled hair or with certain products avoided beforehand.

  • Reference photos if helpful

    For certain styles or color work, your stylist may ask for reference photos in advance to align before the appointment.

  • Deposit instructions when required

    If a deposit is needed, your stylist will send clear instructions. You will not be asked to send payment without knowing where and how.

  • Aftercare before you leave

    Your stylist will walk you through how to maintain your results before your appointment ends.

For Color & Chemical Services

If your appointment involves color, chemical processing, or advanced prep work, your stylist may send you a short intake form before your visit. This is not part of the standard booking process for every client.

  • Full color and highlights
  • Bleach, tone, and color correction
  • Relaxers and chemical straightening
  • Keratin and smoothing treatments
  • Wig color and customization

The form arrives pre-filled with your name, service, and pricing. Your stylist sends it — you do not need to locate it yourself.

How Payment Works

Pricing discussed
before work begins.

Clear before you sit down.

We work to make sure pricing is discussed and confirmed with you before your appointment begins. If the scope of your service changes after consultation, updated pricing is communicated before proceeding.

  • Deposits may be required to secure certain appointments. Your stylist will let you know at booking.
  • Deposit instructions are sent by your stylist — you will not be asked to send payment without confirmation of where and how.
  • The remaining balance is settled at the end of your appointment based on the confirmed service total.
  • If a service changes after consultation, pricing is discussed and agreed before work continues.
